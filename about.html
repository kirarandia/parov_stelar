Marcus Füreder is an Austrian Musician, composer, producer, DJ and designer. The music genre that he produces is a combination of jazz, house, electro, hip hop, and pop.
Parov Stelar won 8 Amadeus Austrian Music Awards
•Electronic/Dance (2012, 2013, 2015 and 2016, 2019)
•Album of the Year for The Princess (2013)
•Best Live Act (2013 and 2014)
The track Booty Swing charted positions in the electronic US and Canadian iTunes charts.
All Night reached double platinum status for 100,000 tracks sold in Italy in May 2017 and in May 2018 the song The Sun featuring Graham Candy reached the No. 1 chart posit ion in the US iTunes Electronic Charts.
